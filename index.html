YouWe

Dreamers of Better India

YouWe is a group of youngsters working with central goal of Gender neutral laws, growth of reasonable sex education and encouraging awareness and helping victims of several distinct crimes.

YouWe was incorporated in July,2022 and is currently working in Bihar, UP, AP, Bengal and started working in multiple states to make our country a safer country. Since the formation of Youwe volunteers have achieved 35 ground campaigns, which include cloth distribution, food distribution, education campaigns, covering government schools, slums, and some centers of other NGOs.
