Welcome to YouWe...

ABOUT US

YOUWE is a group started by very young minds, working together intending to have safer society for every gender and every living being. YouWe believes in Gender Equality. At YouWe,we recognize victim as survivors and provide them justice as well as respect they deserve in society. India's crime rate and statesistics holds topmost rank in the world. Of these, the most significant and violence offence is "Rape"-a crime that needs immediate attention at a societal as well as legal level. For this, we want compulsory sex education in every school-colleges of India.
YouWe believes, "Prevention is better than Cure"and so works on root causes Members of YouWe tend on spreading awareness among the people and helping the survivors of various crimes like rape, false allegation, child abuse, acid attack, etc that are happening every day in the country.

SO WHAT DO WE DO?

  • We conduct seminars in schools to spread awareness among children about good touch and bad touch and also provide sex education to all age group.
  • We conduct awareness campaigns in villages, slum area to educate common people about menstrual hygiene, importance of mental-physical health,need of knowing judiciary laws etc.
  • We personally interact with people to understand psychology of a victim, culprit, ordinary people and work accordingly.
  • We conduct playcard campaigns at various places to aware people about the rapid growth in crime rate, it's seriousness and wats to stop them.
  • We provide foods and colthes to needy, feed animals and stop animal abuse.
  • We all help in natural disasters relief works like flood, earthquake, COVID,etc.
  • Also, we are building a nationwide team to reach out survivors a-d their family for help as soon as possible.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?

We aren't different! You and We all are equal and same. Even we have been through some cruelties of society at some point of our life. And thus, we have taken this initiative to serve and salute the spirit of survivors. We have only just begun and we plan to grow even stronger, ultimately helping the "Dream Of Better India" become a reality.
